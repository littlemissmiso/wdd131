GENERAL CAT CARE

Information Source: https://www.aspca.org/pet-care/cat-care/general-cat-care

Feeding Your Cat

  • Feed your cat 2-3 times per day at regular times.
  • Your cat should eat 1/4-1/3 C per meal, depending on age and size.
  • Use a mix of both wet and dry high quality cat food. Ensure they contain taurine.
  • Have fresh, clean drinking water available at all times.
  • Treats should be no more than 5-10% of the diet.
  • Do not feed your cat table scraps, dairy, or other human consumables.

Grooming Your Cat

  • Brush your cat regularly as needed. (Varies depending on fur length)
  • Only bathe your cat when absolutely necessary.
  • Trim claws regularly, and provide multiple scratching posts.
  • Work to maintain good dental hygeine.

Litter Box

  • You should have 1 litter box per cat, plus one. So 2 cats would need 3 boxes.
  • Place litter boxes in easy to access areas, away from loud noises.
  • Avoid heavily scented litter.
  • Scoop litter at least 1x per day, and fully change litter 1x per month.

General Advice

  • Play with your cat regularly to prevent boredom and encourage bonding.
  • Actively watch for any health concerns.
  • Keep up to date with vaccinations and health checkups.
  • Spay/Neuter your cat.
  • Be careful with allowing young children around your cat. Teach proper handling.

Necessary Cat Supplies

  • Quality cat food
  • Food Dish
  • Water Bowl
  • Interactive Toys
  • Brush
  • Safety Collar with ID Tag
  • Scratching Post or Pad
  • Litter Box & Litter
  • Cat Carrier
  • Cat Bed
