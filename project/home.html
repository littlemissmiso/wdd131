Our Story

Whiskers & Whimsy was born from a deep love for cats and a passion for making a difference. Founded by Emily Harper, a lifelong cat lover, the agency began as a small project from her home. Emily had always dreamed of helping abandoned cats find loving homes, but it wasn‚Äôt until she rescued a stray kitten that she realized how many others needed her help. What started as fostering a few cats quickly grew into a full-fledged adoption agency. Emily's mission is simple: to provide every cat with the care, attention, and love they deserve while matching them with families who will cherish them forever. Through Whiskers & Whimsy, Emily brings together the magic of feline companionship and the joy of adoption, making it a whimsical journey for both cats and their new families. Today, the agency continues to thrive, helping countless cats find their perfect homes, one whisker at a time.
